01

Overview

Hover Quick Preview does not collect, store, or share any personal user data.

The extension operates locally within the user's browser and only accesses product information on supported e-commerce websites to display availability previews (such as sizes and colors).

No personal information, browsing history, or sensitive data is collected or transmitted to any external servers.

02

03

How the Extension Works

Hover Quick Preview operates entirely on-device. Here is exactly what happens when you use it:

1️⃣

You hover a product card

The extension's content script detects the hover event on your page — this happens locally in your browser tab.

2️⃣

Variant data is fetched

To show size and color availability, the extension may make a network request directly to the same website you are already visiting (e.g., a Shopify product JSON endpoint). This is a direct browser-to-website request — identical to what that website's own code would do. No data passes through our servers.

3️⃣

A popup is shown locally

The popup is rendered entirely within your browser using a Shadow DOM. It disappears the moment you move your cursor away. Nothing is stored.

⚡

In-memory cache only

Product variant data may be cached in memory for up to 5 minutes to reduce repeat requests. This cache is cleared automatically when you close or reload the tab. It is never written to disk or sent anywhere.

04

Permissions Explained

The Extension requests the following Chrome permissions. Here is exactly why each one is needed:

💾

storage

Used only to save your enable/disable toggle preference (on or off). One boolean value. Nothing else.

🌍

host_permissions: <all_urls>

Required so the extension can inject the hover listener and fetch product variant data on any shopping website you visit. Without this, the extension would only work on a pre-approved list of domains. We do not use this permission to read, collect, or transmit your browsing activity.

We request only the minimum permissions necessary for the extension to function. We do not request permissions for tabs, cookies, history, identity, or any other sensitive APIs.
05

Third-Party Services

Hover Quick Preview does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data services. We do not share any information with third parties because we do not collect any information to share.

When the extension fetches product data, it communicates directly with the website you are already visiting — not with any server we operate or control.

06

Children's Privacy

Hover Quick Preview does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The extension is a passive browser utility with no accounts, sign-up, or data submission of any kind.

07

Our commitment: Hover Quick Preview will always be a zero-data-collection extension. If that ever changes, you will know before installing any update.
